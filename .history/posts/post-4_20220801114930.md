## Les vacances

My blog is also about my hobbies and what I love doing, travelling is obviously a big component of my lifestyle.

My career trajectory is so atypical, I first worked as a flight attendant and that unique experience, reinforced my open-mnidness and my will to explore and enjoy life to its fullest. Travelling all around the world in a short lapse of time, is such a privilege. Never in a lifetime I would guess that I would travel in Hong Kong 5 times within a month just by requesting thqt destination.

I am fully greatful and know the chance I had. Now today, I still possess that will to travel in unique and awesome places, and that year I chose to go to Miami. That trip was just as I imagined: grandiose, epic, marvellous.

I always had that eager to travel to the United States, being raised with the american soft power since little girl, played a huge part for my love for that country.

The movies, the tv shows, music, art, even food (French readers forgive me ahah), I mean they have it all culturally, I am fond of their lifestyle.

I also notice how nice, relaxed they are, that was quite mind blowing, as I live in a country where everything is going 1555 miles / hour, and everyone is stressed, cold faced, here in Florida they take the time to enjoy life.

You can also notice that time is an interesting component there, by observing the building architectures, they're full of flashy colours, lightened with those 60s' neons, and these old school designs.
It just feels like time has frozen there and you live in a different period, where Elvis may park his Cadillac and show up in Ocean Drive in any minute, or where Miami Vice cops would spectacularly chase some
coke dealers at any moment in their 70s fashionnable suit. Yes the Miami feeling was undescribable.

Okay I said that floridian peope take the time and are pretty relaxed, but is it because is a touristic area, and it is always sunny? NYC might not have the same mentality though. What I can say is that, they are really helpful, cordial, willing to party at any moment (got stopped in the street billions of time by locals, asking me where I'm heading tonight to turn up),
they do get the time to go to the beach, to have little run at sunrise, to exercise at the ocean drive bay with music blasting at 4444 decibels.

They are truely making the time fot things that actually matter: selfcare, taking care of their mental and physical health.

The weather, is just in-cre-di-ble. I had few concerns before going though, feeling like July might not be the best season to travel in Florida, and yet not even one raindrop in several days.

I was strolling around the hotel area in a swimsuit and my beach bag, just the vacation style. That was that kind of details that made my stay there so ethereal.
