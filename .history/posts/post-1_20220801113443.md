## My journey as a web developer

   My life  has always been a synonym of adventures and risk-takings, that is obviously natural to me,
  to purusue a career that is a galaxy away from the profesional path I have been to for years.

Me purposely referring the tech industry with astronomical terms, is explained by the tremendous ocean of tools, languages, sectors, opportunties and core technologies built on daily basis. Internet has been invented only 40 years ago, and yet it is blowing my mind, how it revolutionned on deep levels how way or lives.
Our lives has just drastically improved, having that unlimited access to information, having direct contacts with people accross the world,
collecting essential data, being in the heart of historical events through social medias. All this new ways of communications and informations, are
  mainly the reasons why I am now willing to become a software engineer.

I too, would like to create, to contribute to that important mission to connect people all around the world with specific platforms, and advanced technologic tools.

Always being passionate about creative relative industries, fashion is among one or example. So linking my love for the fashion cosmos with my web developer skillset, would be a big project that I would love to get on with. Though, during the pandemic and endless lockdowns, my idea was to create and design a clothing line for women, drawing my own line and buying the fabrics of my own choosing and then sew them together. That ambitious project, faced few blocks along the way: indeed the fashion industry offers multiple opportunities but on the production level, it is easy to draw design create during the early phases of creations, contact supplier to invest in specific fabrics etc; the real challenge though is way higher, your line after being created sewed and came into life, has to be recognized known by your customer, fashion peers to make the sells. So the key here is to ge the rght connexions and have some marketing knowledge to put your clothing line into the spotlight.

Also the work of creating and produce the clothes myself would have been a huge amount of time, energy, money to invest in. Laser focused and adamant to the idea that I will make it, I already invested into a brand new Juki sewing maching costing a lot, and felt like that was it for me I will find a way to become a successful fashion designer. But in reality, without any connexions with high profile professionals in the industry, and the right adress book, and only betting myself on my vision and skillset, would have been a too huge risk to take, so I sadly gave up, or let's say press a pause button on that dream project of mine. Yes very ambitious, I am, and I have always seen things in B-i-G. Pushing my 100% self into projects, ideas, visions, until it is time to make them happen, meaning money investment or getting the contacts that'll help me get these ideas into life.

That time, being a web developer and pursuing a successful career into the tech industry, is not out of reach. I do not have to fill my adress book with Mark Zuckerber contact details, that time is about showing your skills, proving how good you are at programming, and demonstrate how passionate you be doing it.

I am fueled with motivation and hunger, to succeed and why not attach these two passions of mine in one. I also see myself injecting, some algorithm, artificial intelligence and machine
learning programs into it. Giving the user an unique exprience where she/he is will be able to find the right outfit, dependending to her/his body size, weather/season, mood, context: professional? vacations? date?

I know I get little bit ahead of myself (things I cannot help myself to do 😜). Let'see that ambitious project is lieing somewhere in a corner of my head, where are definately not here yet.

But I am already, piling ideas over ideas for that project, writing them down in a to-do list,so when I have enough confidence and tech skills to build it I'll just take a leap.
Now it is just about advancing into my engineering career.
